YCA Projects

Faith in action through innovation and empowerment

National Bible Competitions

Churches and schools form teams of 10 to compete in annual Bible competitions, practicing all year using the YCA Bible Quiz website. The goal: spread the Word through learning and fellowship.

Infinity Access

An app that provides unlimited daily internet access at 2000 UGX using VPS and WireGuard technology — bridging digital gaps for youth and schools affordably.

YCA School

An online secondary school platform with notes, quizzes, AI-powered tutoring, and progress tracking — enabling every student to learn and grow anytime, anywhere.

YCA Hotspots

We set up public Wi-Fi hotspots across Ugandan regions with captive portals to provide affordable access and promote YCA’s educational and faith-driven platforms.

Tech Gen

An online platform dedicated to teaching programming and tech courses — empowering Christian youth with digital skills for the modern world.

MLOps Engineer Training

Training and mentoring MLOps engineers with hands-on cloud deployment skills — preparing them for global tech jobs and empowering digital missionaries.

Data Entry AI

An AI-powered camera app that converts handwritten documents into digital templates — revolutionizing data entry in schools and institutions.

Handwritten Bible

A national faith initiative where each person handwrites one verse — creating a collective handwritten Bible, symbolizing unity and devotion across Uganda.
