YCA Future Prospects

Our vision for the next generation of Christian youth

YCA Tour Bus

We envision a modern tour bus designed to carry youth across the nation for outreach, missions, and tourism. The YCA bus will represent unity, mobility, and evangelism — reaching schools, communities, and churches across borders.

YCA Tower

The YCA Tower will be a beacon of light — housing tech labs, Bible study halls, and administrative offices. It will stand as a symbol of faith-driven innovation and a hub for youth empowerment.

International Partnerships

We aim to collaborate with Christian organizations and sponsors abroad to strengthen outreach, education, and digital evangelism through global cooperation.

YCA Secondary School

We plan to establish a physical Christian secondary school that integrates faith, technology, and moral leadership — raising a generation grounded in both wisdom and innovation.

Tech Jobs and Innovation

Through our training and partnerships, YCA seeks to connect skilled youth to tech jobs, startup opportunities, and mentorship programs — building a Christian-driven digital economy.
